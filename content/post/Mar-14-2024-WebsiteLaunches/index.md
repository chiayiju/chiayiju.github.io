After a period of long waiting. Our research group website has finally launched.

<!--more-->

We are a group of physicists dedicated to unraveling the underlying mechanisms of Nature through mathematics and logic (and, of course, a bit of educated guessing). By a happy coincidence, or perhaps pure luck, our group’s website officially launches on $\pi$ Day in 2024.

Our research spans various fields in physics, from classical to quantum and ''mathematical'' to ''physical''. Through this website, we invite you to get acquainted with us and our work. We plan to update the site with our latest research findings and upcoming events from time to time.

Finally, the primary goal of publishing site is to make our research more accessible to potential visitors, collaborators, and students. You can find our contact information in the 'Contact' section or reach out to individual members through the 'People' section.

Formal Theory
Research Group

The Formal Theory Research Group at National Sun Yat-sen University aims to tackle various problems in theoretical physics with mathematical and geometrical tools.

Our research group is mainly interested in the mathematical structures in physics. We explore varied areas in theoretical physics, from quantum formalism and superstring theories to quantum information and computing. Specifically, we study the geometrical structures in these fields, aiming to deepen our understanding of theoretical physics and translate it into practical applications.

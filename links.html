Useful notes/books:

• David Tong's lecture notes

- Description: Great lecture notes. I strongly recommend them to any student interested in theoretical physics.

• Fields by Warren Siegel

- Description: The best free textbook on the modern approach to quantum field theory and introductory string theory to date.

- The errata and the incomplete new version can be found here

• Superspace by James Gates, Marcus Grisaru, Martin Roček, and Warren Siegel

- Description: The most comprehensive free book on superfield approach to supersymmetric field theories to date.

• Introduction to String Field Theory by Warren Siegel

- Description: A free book on string field theory (the second quantized string theory).
